----------------------------------------------------------------
1. GETTING STARTED
----------------------------------------------------------------

INSTALLATION:
  1. Make sure Python 3.7+ is installed on your machine
  2. Open your terminal or command prompt
  3. Navigate to the liggs folder:
       cd path/to/liggs
  4. Install the required dependency:
       pip install flask
  5. Run the app:
       python app.py
  6. Open your browser and go to:
       http://localhost:5000

That's it! The app is now running locally on your machine.
The database (liggs.db) is created automatically on first run.


----------------------------------------------------------------
2. INTRO ANIMATION
----------------------------------------------------------------

When you first open Liggs, you'll see:
  - A dark star field
  - A UFO flying in from the top of the screen
  - The UFO hovering with glowing colored lights
  - The UFO rocketing away
  - The main Liggs app fading in smoothly

This animation plays every time you open or refresh the page.


----------------------------------------------------------------
3. CREATING AN ACCOUNT
----------------------------------------------------------------

  1. After the animation, you'll see the Sign In screen
  2. Click the "Register" tab
  3. Enter a username of your choice
  4. Enter a password (minimum 4 characters)
  5. Click "Create Account"
  6. You'll be logged in automatically

Your account keeps your notes private and separate from
other users on the same app.


----------------------------------------------------------------
4. LOGGING IN
----------------------------------------------------------------

  1. On the Sign In screen, enter your username and password
  2. Click "Enter the Void"
  3. You'll be taken directly to your notes

To log out, click the "‚Ü™ Out" button in the top right corner.


----------------------------------------------------------------
5. CREATING A NOTE
----------------------------------------------------------------

  Option A: Click the "+ New Note" button in the sidebar
  Option B: Press Ctrl + N on your keyboard

A blank note will open in the editor with:
  - A title field at the top
  - A large writing area below


----------------------------------------------------------------
6. WRITING & EDITING A NOTE
----------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Click on the title field and type your note title
  2. Click on the writing area and start typing your content
  3. Your note is saved automatically after 2 seconds
     of inactivity (you'll see "‚óè saved" in the top bar)
  4. You can also manually save by:
       - Clicking the "Save" button
       - Pressing Ctrl + S on your keyboard


----------------------------------------------------------------
7. SWITCHING BETWEEN NOTES
----------------------------------------------------------------

  - All your notes appear in the sidebar on the left
  - Each note shows the title, a short preview, and the
    last updated time
  - Click any note in the sidebar to open it
  - Your current note auto-saves before switching


----------------------------------------------------------------
8. SEARCHING NOTES
----------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Click the search bar at the top of the screen
  2. Start typing ‚Äî results filter in real time
  3. The sidebar updates instantly showing matching notes
  4. Clear the search bar to see all notes again

Search works across both note titles and note content.


----------------------------------------------------------------
9. DELETING A NOTE
----------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Open the note you want to delete
  2. Click the "‚úï Delete" button in the top right of the editor
  3. Confirm the deletion in the popup dialog
  4. The note is permanently removed

Warning: Deletion cannot be undone.


----------------------------------------------------------------
10. EXPORTING A NOTE
----------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Open the note you want to export
  2. Click the "‚Üì Export" button in the editor toolbar
  3. A .txt file will download automatically to your device

The exported file includes:
  - Note title
  - Creation date
  - Last updated date
  - Full note content

  ‚ú¶ Auto-save kicks in 2 seconds after you stop typing ‚Äî

----------------------------------------------------------------
13. TROUBLESHOOTING
----------------------------------------------------------------

App won't start?
  ‚Üí Make sure Flask is installed: pip install flask
  ‚Üí Try: python3 app.py instead of python app.py

Port already in use?
  ‚Üí Open app.py and change the last line to:
      app.run(debug=True, port=5001)
  ‚Üí Then visit: http://localhost:5001

Can't log in?
  ‚Üí Double-check your username and password
  ‚Üí Passwords are case-sensitive

Lost your notes?
  ‚Üí Make sure liggs.db exists in the liggs folder
  ‚Üí Do not delete liggs.db ‚Äî it stores all your data
